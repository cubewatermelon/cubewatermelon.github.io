Population inversion ensures that the probability of stimulated emission (giving gain) remains higher than that of stimulated absorption.
Ions in the lower level can absorb energy to become ions in the upper state. The probability of this occurring is weighted by the absorption cross section, σa(λ)\sigma_a(\lambda)σa​(λ). Similarly, ions in the upper level can be stimulated to return to the lower level, releasing a photon by stimulated emission. The probability of this occurring is weighted by the emission cross section, σe(λ)\sigma_e(\lambda)σe​(λ).Ions in the upper level can also return to the lower level spontaneously, releasing a photon by spontaneous emission. The probability of spontaneous emission is proportional to the minverse of the lifetime in the upper state.

The spectral dependence of amplifier gain is a weighted combination of the gain and absorption cross sections where the weighting depends on the populations of the upper and lower levels.

Doped Fiber

The model is based on the bidirectional propagation equations for signals and multilevel rate equations for ion populations. The emission and absorption spectra can be specified in terms of cross sections or Giles parameters. The model includes erbium/ytterbium energy transfer, cross-relaxation effects, homogeneous upconversion, pair-induced quenching (except thulium-doped amplifier), excited-state absorption, homogeneous and inhomogeneous broadening, Rayleigh scattering, and Kerr nonlinearity. The model is resolved in both the longitudinal and transverse directions.
The gain and absorption characteristics of the dopant ions within the glass host as a function of optical frequency can be specified using cross sections or Giles parameters. The Giles parameters, which represent the gain and absorption of the fiber per unit length, are generally easier to obtain experimentally and are often provided by manufacturers. The relationship between the gain and absorption per unit length and cross sections can be reached.
Each saturation factor should correspond to the lifetime of spontaneous transition between levels.

Transient Dynamics of a Single EDFA

16 channels are transmitted. At the time moment t=0.2 ms the channels 9-16 are dropped. The decreased EDFA input power due to the channel dropping leads to an increase of the inversion level (for constant pumping) followed by an increase of the output power of the remaining channels. At the time moment t=0.7 ms the channels 9-16 are restored. This causes a second power transient followed by restoration of all output channel powers to the initial state. Duration of both drop and restore events is 0.1 ms.

瞬态效应

工作在实际通信线路上的EDFA,在输入光功率突然变大/变小的时刻,如果泵浦输出功率不能及时跟随输入变化而变小/变大,则掺铒光纤( Erbium Doped Fibe,EDF)中反转离子数仍保持在变化前的水平,会导致剩余通道的增益突然变小/变大,这种增益跃变经多级EDFA级联而积累,会引起线路阻塞或产生误码。如果输入光功率迅速增大,则产生时的光浪涌,经级联放大后,峰值功率可达数瓦,可能损坏通信设备的光/电(O/E)转换器及光连接器端面。

超短脉冲激光器

被动锁模掺饵光纤激光器

在净色散为正的色散管理激光器中，通过调节激光器的参数，如泵浦功率和偏振条件，可以在同一台激光器中输出色散管理孤子，自相似子及耗散孤子。不同的脉冲种类可以通过光谱进行区分。
通过色散补偿技术，成功地在通信波段设计出全光纤耗散孤子超短脉冲光纤激光器，脉宽达到了 78.9 fs，能量 4.6 nJ，峰值功率超过 50 kW，该峰值功率可以和普通的啁啾放大系统相比拟。
实验得到了 128 fs 的超短脉冲，这是在光纤负色散区得到的最短脉冲。得益于脉冲的超短脉宽，虽然激光器工作在负色散区，但是脉冲没有凯利边带。

光纤激光器的被动锁模方法

光纤激光器不仅可以利用传统的锁模方法实现锁模例如饱和吸收体等，还可以利用强的非线性效应如自相位调制，交叉相位调制，构成非线性偏振旋转机制锁模。
基本原理：假设初始脉冲是线偏振态，通过一个四分之一波片后变为椭圆偏振态，椭圆偏振光可以认为是强度不同的两个偏振方向正交的线性偏振分量的叠加。这两个偏振分量在光纤中传输时，由于受到光纤的非线性效应作用（包括自相位调制和交叉相位调制），产生大小不同的非线性相移，从而使脉冲偏振态发生变化。由于偏振态的变化和光强有关，所以脉冲中心部分的变化量大于两翼处，再调节半波片使得脉冲中心的高强度部分能透过起偏器，则两翼的低强度部分会被起偏器阻止，这样就形成了等效的可饱和吸收体，从而可以实现锁模功能。
光纤激光器中的脉冲特性受非线性及色散的影响很大。金兹堡-朗道方程是在非线性薛定谔方程的基础上，增加一些激光器内的物理效应，如增益饱和，饱和吸收体，及其它高阶项等，该方程的实用条件是假设脉冲在腔内的变化不大：
