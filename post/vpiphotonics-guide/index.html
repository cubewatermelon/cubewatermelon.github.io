FiberNLS_PMD

The module solves a system of two coupled nonlinear Schroedinger equations describing the propagation of two orthogonal polarization components of optical signals in fibers with randomly varying birefringence, and hence allows statistical modeling of polarization mode dispersion (PMD), including biasing the statistics for a high differential group delay.
The model takes into account ** polarization-dependent loss (PDL)** and all orders of PMD, four-wave mixing (FWM), self-phase modulation (SPM), cross-phase modulation (XPM), first-order group velocity dispersion (GVD), second order GVD, and attenuation of the fiber. The adjustment factors for SPM, FWM, and XPM due to polarization evolution are automatically included.

SNR的计算

针对具有随机非正交双偏振的真实光纤，需要利用琼斯矩阵进行反解。
通过EVM（信号误差矢量：指接收到的信号与理想信号之间误差矢量的幅度）计算SNR。
